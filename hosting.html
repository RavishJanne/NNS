For homeowners

Turn a spare closet into a grid credit.

If your home can spare a few square feet, a dedicated circuit, and a stable connection, it can host a piece of the grid.

01

Apply

Tell us your address, panel capacity, and connection speed. We check line-of-sight to the nearest Block Hub in under a minute.

02

Install

A technician mounts the enclosure, runs the dedicated circuit, and connects the heat-capture line if your setup supports it. Most installs take under two hours.

03

Earn

Your Home Node joins the mesh and starts contributing compute. You get a monthly grid credit, plus a heating offset once the temperature drops.

Typical requirements

  • 9 sq ft of clearance
  • Dedicated 20A circuit
  • 100+ Mbps symmetric
  • Indoor temp 50–85°F
